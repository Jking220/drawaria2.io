How to Play Drawaria2.io

1

Join or Create a Room

Enter a public room or create your own private game with friends using a room code.

2

Take Turns Drawing

When it's your turn, you'll get a word to draw. Use the drawing tools to create your masterpiece.

3

Guess the Word

While others are drawing, type your guesses in the chat. The faster you guess, the more points you earn!

4

Earn Points & Rewards

Collect points for correct guesses and when your drawings are guessed. Top players win cash prizes!
